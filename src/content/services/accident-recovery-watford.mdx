## Specialist Recovery for Damaged Vehicles

When Prompt Recovery arrives at the scene, the focus is on a controlled, professional
recovery. Accident damage often means a car cannot be driven, which is where our
specialized equipment comes into play.

- **Heavy-Duty Winching**: Our recovery truck is equipped with a powerful winch
designed to pull immobilized vehicles onto the bed with total control. Whether the car
is stuck in gear or has suffered wheel damage, the winch handles the heavy lifting to
ensure the vehicle is cleared from the road quickly.
- **Using Skates for a Smooth Load**: If a collision has resulted in a snapped axle, a
seized wheel, or an electronic handbrake that won't release, we utilize skates
(dollies). By placing these under the wheels, we ensure that as the winch pulls the
car, it glides onto our bed rather than being dragged roughly across the tarmac. This
protects the tyres, suspension, and the vehicle's frame from further stress during
loading.
- **Vehicle Recovery & Secure Towing**: We provide a stable, professional transport
solution. We can move your car or van directly to a trusted local garage, your home
address, or a location of your choice.
- **Motorway Recovery**: For incidents on the **M1** or **M25**, we prioritise the call.
High-speed roads require a fast, visible recovery presence to ensure the vehicle is
cleared immediately, reducing the risk of secondary accidents.
