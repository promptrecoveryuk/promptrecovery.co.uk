## What To Do While You Wait: Our Advice

Safety is the absolute priority on a motorway. Before we even arrive, it is vital to ensure the situation is managed correctly to protect you and other road users.

1. **Don't Panic**: It's easier said than done, but staying calm helps you make better decisions for your safety.
2. **Exit Left**: If the car is still moving, try to get it to the hard shoulder or an emergency refuge area.
3. **Get Out and Stay Out**: Exit the vehicle through the left-hand doors (away from traffic). Move behind the crash barrier and stay well back from the road, ideally moving up the bank if there is one.
4. **Stay Visible**: Keep the car's sidelights and hazard lights on.
5. **Call the Experts**: Once you are in a safe spot, call Prompt Recovery. We will talk you through the next steps and give you a precise arrival time for the recovery truck.
