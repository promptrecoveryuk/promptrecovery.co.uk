Reaching 250+ 5-star Google reviews is a big moment for Prompt Recovery, but the number only tells part of the story.

Behind every review is someone who needed help at a stressful time. A car would not start. A van could not be driven.
A vehicle needed moving safely from the roadside, a driveway, a garage, or a busy local road. In those moments, people
are not looking for complicated promises. They want someone to pick up the phone, give clear information, agree a fair
price, and handle the recovery properly.

That is what Prompt Recovery has tried to build from the start: a practical local recovery service for drivers in
Watford and the surrounding areas, with straightforward communication and careful vehicle transport.

To mark the milestone, we asked Nick, the Founder of Prompt Recovery, a few questions about the reviews, the work, and what customers can expect when
they call.

## What was going through your mind when Prompt Recovery started getting its first reviews?

Honestly I was just happy. It showed people were actually satisfied with the service —
that they got where they needed to go safely, and took the time to leave a review. It means
a lot.

## What do customers usually need most when they call?

Most of the time it's recovery — taking the car home or to a garage. And then the second
most would be accident recovery. I deal with both quite a lot depending on the situation.

## What should someone expect when they call you after breaking down?

I'll ask where they are and the postcode of where the car needs to go. If the customer is
willing to share their location, that helps the process. Then I check traffic and let them know
how long I'll be, and finally I'll give them a quote. Trying to keep it simple and clear from the start.

## What is one memorable job you've done?

To be fair, I remember all my jobs… but one that stood out to me was a car that actually
lost a wheel on the motorway. The wheel fully came off while driving. Luckily it wasn't as bad
as it could've been, but yeah… that one stayed with me.

## Is there a piece of feedback you see again and again?

A lot of people say they'd recommend me, which is good to see. And quite a few mention
that I made the whole situation easier for them and that's exactly what I'm aiming for,
especially in a stressful moment.

## What standards do you try to keep on every callout?

Just keeping it professional but also genuine. Turning up when I say I will, keeping people
updated, and making sure the car's handled properly. Just doing things properly every time.
