# Local SEO Backlog For Trello

Current snapshot: April 22, 2026

## Parsing Contract

- Each Trello card starts with a heading in the format `### Card: <title>`.
- Each card contains these metadata lines after a blank line below the heading:
  - `Card ID: <stable-id>`
  - `List: Inbox 📥`
  - `Labels: label 1, label 2, label 3`
- The body of each card uses these exact section headings:
  - `#### What are we trying to achieve?`
  - `#### Why are we doing this?`
  - `#### Acceptance criteria`
- `Acceptance criteria` is always a flat bullet list.

## Guardrails

- The homepage should remain the primary page for `breakdown recovery watford`.
- The Watford breakdown service page should support the homepage, not compete with it.
- Area pages should be genuinely local and believable, not thin templates.
- Service pages should stay accurate to recovery and towing. Do not imply full roadside repair if that is not offered.
- Do not claim `24/7` if the real operating hours remain Monday to Saturday only.
